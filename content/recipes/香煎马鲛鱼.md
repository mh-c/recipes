## 食材

- 马鲛鱼 2-3 块

**酱料（可选）**

- 煎完后可调制，目前无推荐配方

## 步骤

1. 马鲛鱼洗净擦干水分，
2. Toss tofu into a bowl of cornstarch and coat evenly
3. Prepare sauce by combining rice vinegar, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, water, sugar & cornstarch
